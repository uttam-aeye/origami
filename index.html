Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a pharmacy/diagnostic/imaging service at the clinic?

A: The clinic offers dental imaging services.

Q: How many patients can be seated in the waiting area?

A: Approximately 9-10 patients can be accommodated at a time.

Q: What digital payment modes are accepted?

A: Paytm and Google Pay are accepted.

Q: Do I need an appointment before visiting?

A: Yes, appointments are required to ensure timely consultation.

Q: What is the follow-up consultation fee and duration?

A: The follow-up fee is ₹200; duration depends on the treatment.

Q: Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

A: No, the clinic does not have a wheelchair accessible entrance.

Q: How can I book an appointment?

A: Appointments can be booked online through our website or via Practo.

Q: What specialties do your doctors offer?

A: Our doctors specialize in general dentistry, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and more.

Q: What are the clinic timings?

A: Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM; Sunday: Closed.

Q: Where is the clinic located?
